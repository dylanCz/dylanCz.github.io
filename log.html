April-June

Updates

  • Well, Pokemon Champions is interesting...
    I was quite looking forward to see what Nintendo would cook for the official battle simulator, and while I think the general idea was well-founded, I can't help but feel the execution and decision making is poor.
    Pros:
    • The animations are nice
    • It's so much faster than S/V, tournaments will be significantly nicer to watch
    • It's free to play, and gaining points is fairly trivial if you battle a lot
    Cons:
    • No replay support
    • No teamsheet support and ranked is best of 1
    • Many expected pokemon and items are missing
    Not going to write it off just yet but definitely a little bit disappointing, will see if Nintendo pull anything out of the bag over the next few months.

Updates

  • I've started taking my SSBM practise a little bit more seriously. After some disappointment of not being on the Scottish PR and poor tournaments results in 2025, I want to take a push this year to see how far I can go. For now I'm focused on playing more high level opponents, analysing my gameplay more often and watching higher level falcos.
